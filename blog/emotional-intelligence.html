What is Emotional Intelligence?

EQ is essentially emotional intelligence which refers to a person's ability to navigate their emotions for example someone who is aware of their emotions and has empathy for others usually has a high eq compared to someone who cannot control and manage their emotions easily so they easily resort to anger and negative emotions. Eq is basically about understanding oneself, recognizing and managing your feelings and using this emotional awareness to interact with others on a truly emotional level so a person with a high eq is emotionally aware and very skilled at managing both their own emotions and emotional relationships with others and they know how to express their feelings effectively which creates meaningful bonds.

How is EQ different to IQ?

On the other hand IQ which stands for intelligence quotient measures a person's intelligence in terms of focus, problem-solving, and critical thinking. Unlike eq which focuses highly on how a person can manage their emotional self, iq is more focused on how someone can analyse situations and information. Both eq and iq are crucial for a person's success even though they concentrate on different aspects of the mind.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important because it helps you understand yourself at a deeper level and others as well it also helps in managing all of your relationships including the one with yourself. Developing a high eq can help you handle different aspects of your life such as work stress, exams, and relationships. With a high eq you can learn to master your emotions making it easier to tackle any challenge that comes your way as you will know how to deal with your emotions when things get tough. Having a high eq can lead you to live a more furfilling and happier life.
